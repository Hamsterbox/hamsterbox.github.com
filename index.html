Our mission

We are creating a personal cloud device that respects your digital rights and privacy while providing you with the comfort of cloud software.

We believe you should have a choice that isn't between digital surveillance and missing out on comfort and technology.

Our software and hardware is beautiful and convenient, sustainable and open. Your device is yours and we won't interfere.

We value simplicity, so we do the heavy lifting for you so all you have to do is plug & play.
